✅ Problem Statement (Simplified)
You are given an array of strings (can be names, words, etc.). You need to find the second most frequently occurring string.

Input:
N = 6
arr[] = {"aaa", "bbb", "ccc", "bbb", "aaa", "aaa"}

Output:
"bbb"
Explanation:
"aaa" → occurs 3 times
"bbb" → occurs 2 times
"ccc" → occurs 1 time
So, second most frequent = "bbb"
